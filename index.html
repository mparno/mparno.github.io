Red Road Workshop, LLC

Giving vintage parts and eclectic things a new life

At the Red Road Workshop our goal is to create intriguing objects, creatures, and displays from varied components. We sometimes rescue vintage parts of history and othertimes we incorporate the eclectic. When we start to create a piece, we never know where it is going to take us; the components themselves set the direction.
